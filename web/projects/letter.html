letter from a pier

Letter From a Pier (8:04’) is a short experimental essay about memory and women. Cold autumn in Estonia is turning into a winter, which brings wondering around last year. The film is made from footage filmed during one year. It is a diary film, developed through traveling, loving, getting home, abandoning. The film was screened a few times in Prague, at Famufest, in Národní Galerie, and at art universities.
