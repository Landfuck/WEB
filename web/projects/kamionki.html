RESIDENCY
collaboration with koło gospodyń wiejskich april 2024

During the residency, I chose to collaborate with a local "housewife club" (Koło Gospodyń Wiejskich), along with a few mothers and their children. My intention wasn’t necessarily to produce work for exhibition, but rather to create a space where we could enjoy ourselves, experiment, and explore hidden corners of our creativity. Together, we engaged in playful exercises—drawing while blindfolded, action painting on a large 3×4 meter canvas, and attempting to capture the shapes and colors of things we couldn’t see. The outcomes were meant to be light-hearted and joyful. Through this process, I hoped to show that creativity is for everyone, and that it can help us release, connect, and simply be present.

Blindfolded drawing workshop was part of the process.

With some helpf from rain and wind we created large format painting.

The works were shown at the end of the redidency. Important part of it was a dinner and a Karaoke by Jagoda Dobecka.
